# **⭐️ Welcome!**

Thank you for participating in Turbo AI’s hiring process! This challenge is designed to evaluate your technical skills, problem-solving approach, and creativity under constraints. By the end, we hope to gain a deeper understanding of your capabilities.

We’re looking forward to seeing your work and hearing your thought process!

# **✍️ Key Constraints**

- **Tech Stack**: The app must be built using **Django/Django Rest** (backend) and **Next.js** (frontend).
- **AI:** We welcome and encourage you to use **AI tools** to enhance and speed up your process. Please, let us know on your README, how did you use it.

# 💯 **Criteria**

We’ll assess your work based on the following:

1. **Functionality**: Does the app meet the outlined requirements?
2. **Code Quality**: Is the code clean, maintainable and has good coverage?
3. **Creativity**: Did you approach the problem innovatively or use AI tools effectively?
4. **Time Management**: How well did you prioritize tasks within the time constraint?

# 📤 Deliverables

When you’re done, send us a link to your **GitHub repository** containing:

1. **Source Code**: The complete codebase for your project in a public GitHub repo.
2. **README File**:
    - A summary of your process.
    - Key design and technical decisions.
    - The AI tools you used and how did you use them?
